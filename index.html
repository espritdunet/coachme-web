Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2026
CoachMe is built on the principle of Privacy by Design. Your inspiration is your business, not ours.

🔒 100% Offline & Private

CoachMe runs entirely locally. No accounts, no telemetry, and no hidden analytics (like Firebase or Google Analytics). We don't even know when you open the app.

💾 Local Storage

Everything is stored on your Mac using SwiftData and UserDefaults. Your custom images remain within the App's secure sandbox.

🛠 Technical Transparency

We use Apple's Unified Logging (OSLog) for diagnostics. All sensitive content (your card text and file paths) is explicitly marked as <private>. This means it is automatically redacted and never visible in system logs unless you are debugging the app yourself.

🔄 Local Backups

Automatic backups are created before major updates, but they never leave your machine. They are stored in your Application Support folder and can be managed directly from the app settings.

🌐 Third-Party Services

The app does not communicate with any external servers. The only third-party interaction is with standard macOS services (like iCloud if you manually choose to sync your files).

Since we collect no data, we do not track or profile users, including children under 13. For any privacy-related questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
